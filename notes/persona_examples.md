# Prompt structure for different personas

## General Prompt Structure

```plaintext
Generate a [Content Type] for [Platform] in the style of a [Persona]. The target audience is [Age Group] with an interest in [Interest]. The content should be written for [Intelligence Level] readers with a [Understanding Level] approach. Maintain a [Tone] and ensure it is culturally adapted for [Region]. End with a [Call-to-Action].
```

## 1️⃣ Template for Technical & Educational Content

```plaintext
Generate a [Explainer/Informational/How-To] article for [Platform] in the style of a [Technical Expert / Educator]. 
- The target audience is [Age Group] with an interest in [Interest].  
- Write for [Beginner/Intermediate/Advanced] readers with a [Basic Awareness/Comprehensive] approach.  
- Maintain a [Neutral/Formal/Engaging] tone and ensure it is culturally adapted for [Region].  
- Structure the content with headings, subheadings, and bullet points for clarity.  
- End with a [CTA based on engagement or learning].  
```
### ✅ Example Prompt

```plaintext
Generate a How-To guide for LinkedIn in the style of a Technical Expert.

The target audience is young professionals (18-35) with an interest in AI and Machine Learning.
Write for intermediate readers with a comprehensive approach.
Maintain a neutral and informative tone.
Ensure cultural adaptation for a global audience.
End with a CTA encouraging further exploration and discussion.
```

## 2️⃣ Template for Engaging & Storytelling Content

```plaintext
Write a [Storytelling/Narrative] style post for [Platform] in the style of a [Storyteller/Social Media Influencer].  
- The target audience is [Age Group] with an interest in [Interest].  
- Write for [Intelligence Level] readers with a [Basic Awareness/Detailed] approach.  
- Use engaging language, relatable anecdotes, and emotional appeal.  
- Maintain a [Casual/Inspirational/Conversational] tone.  
- End with a [CTA to engage, share, or comment].  
```

### ✅ Example Prompt

```plaintext
Write a narrative-driven Twitter thread in the style of a Storyteller.

The target audience is teens and young adults (16-30) with an interest in entrepreneurship.
Write for beginner readers with a basic awareness approach.
Use engaging, fun, and motivational language with personal anecdotes.
Maintain a casual and inspiring tone.
End with a CTA asking readers to share their biggest business lesson.
```

## 3️⃣ Template for Marketing & Sales Copy

```plaintext
Write a [Marketing/Promotional] post for [Platform] in the style of a [Persuader/Marketer].  
- The target audience is [Age Group] interested in [Interest].  
- Write for [Intelligence Level] readers with a [Understanding Level] approach.  
- Use persuasive techniques, highlight benefits, and create urgency.  
- Maintain a [Persuasive/Actionable] tone.  
- End with a [Strong Conversion CTA].  
```

### ✅ Example Prompt

```plaintext
Write an Instagram ad copy in the style of a Marketer.

The target audience is young professionals (20-35) interested in personal finance and investment.
Write for intermediate readers with a comprehensive yet engaging approach.
Use persuasive techniques, highlight benefits, and create a sense of urgency.
Maintain an action-driven tone.
End with a strong CTA directing users to sign up for a free consultation.
```

## 4️⃣ Template for Social Media Content
```plaintext

- The target audience is [Age Group] with an interest in [Interest].  
- Write for [Intelligence Level] readers with a [Basic Awareness/Detailed] approach.  
- Keep it short, engaging, and easily shareable.

The target audience is tech enthusiasts (18-40) interested in AI and automation.

Keep it short, engaging, and tweet-friendly.

```

## 6️⃣ Template for Explainers & Educational Threads

```plaintext
Write an [Explainer] for [Platform] in the style of a [Educator/Trainer].  
- The target audience is [Age Group] interested in [Interest].  
- Write for [Intelligence Level] readers with a [Basic/Comprehensive] approach.  
- Break it down step by step with examples and analogies.  
- Maintain a [Neutral/Educational] tone.  
- End with a [CTA directing to further learning resources].  
```

### ✅ Example Prompt

```plaintext
Write an educational explainer thread for Twitter in the style of an Educator.

The target audience is beginners (18-40) interested in AI and LLMs.
Write for beginner readers with a step-by-step breakdown approach.
Use simple analogies and examples.
Maintain an educational but engaging tone.
End with a CTA linking to a free AI learning resource.
```
